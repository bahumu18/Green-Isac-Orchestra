About Us

Green Isac Orchestra is a Norwegian music group, which was originally started by Andreas Eriksen and Morten Lund in 1990, under the name Green Isac. In 2007, the group expanded to an instrumental live quintet with Tov Ramstad, Frode Larsen and Jo Wang.


For some years, the Green Isac Orchestra has had a concert series at Kampen Bistro in Oslo under the name Etnotronica. Green IsacOrchestra A Norwegian instrumental band blending electronic and ethnic instruments alike into kinetic rhythms, sometimes electronically fueled, and often acoustically driven, with accents from processed guitars and quirky synths, setting it all in otherworldly landscapes with an air of mystery and intrigue. «One of the greatest progressive music acts in Scandinavia.» -Angel Romero -PROGRESSIVE ROCK CENTRAL.
