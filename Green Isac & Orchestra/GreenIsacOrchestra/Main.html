NEWS

Jeff Greinke’s fourth album for Spotted Peccary Music delivers elegant modern classical, electronic, and ambient atmospheres.Portland, OR (February 19, 2021) — Composer and soundscape artist Jeff Greinke presents an elegant musical landscape of delicate compositions and harmonically rich ambience, on his album titled Other Weather. The eleven slowly-unfolding tracks offer multi-layered textures that weave acoustic and electronic instrumentation into a gossamer soundscape that shimmers with the stillness of a perfectly calm atmosphere in the warm light of early dusk. Other Weather, on the Spotted Peccary Music label, releases today in physical CD format and in 24-BIT AUDIOPHILE, CD QUALITY LOSSLESS, MP3 and streaming formats. Find sales and streaming links at https://spottedpeccary.com/shop/other-weather/.

As with 2018’s Before Sunrise, Other Weather spans the genres of modern classical, electronic, and ambient as it gently evolves through a refined set of impressionistic ambient chamber music. Blending electronic ambiences and effects with an acoustic ensemble that includes cello, viola, violin, French horn, clarinets, flutes, and small percussion, Greinke realizes his musical vision through an empirical process of improvisation and experimentation, combining tracks and layering sounds, and uncovering the magical moments as they reveal themselves.

ABOUT US

Green Isac Orchestra is a Norwegian music group, which was originally started by Andreas Eriksen and Morten Lund in 1990, under the name Green Isac. In 2007, the group expanded to an instrumental live quintet with Tov Ramstad, Frode Larsen and Jo Wang. For some years, the Green Isac Orchestra has had a concert series at Kampen Bistro in Oslo under the name Etnotronica.

Green IsacOrchestra A Norwegian instrumental band blending electronic and ethnic instruments alike into kinetic rhythms, sometimes electronically fueled, and often acoustically driven, with accents from processed guitars and quirky synths, setting it all in otherworldly landscapes with an air of mystery and intrigue. «One of the greatest progressive music acts in Scandinavia.» -Angel Romero -PROGRESSIVE ROCK CENTRAL.
